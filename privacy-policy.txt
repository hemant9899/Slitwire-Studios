Privacy Policy for Aura Edge AI

  Last updated: March 23, 2026

  Aura Edge AI processes all data on your device. No photos, analysis results,
  or personal data are uploaded to any server.

  Camera permission is used for photo capture within the app. Photos are
  processed locally and never transmitted.

  Optional analytics (opt-in only) tracks app usage patterns — no photo
  content or personal data is collected.

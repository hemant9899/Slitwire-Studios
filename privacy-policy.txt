# Privacy Policy for Aura Edge AI

**Last Updated: March 23, 2026**

## Overview

Aura Edge AI ("the App") is a privacy-first AI photo analysis application. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. No photos, analysis results, or personal data are uploaded to any external server.

## Data Collection

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Photos or images from your device
- AI analysis results, descriptions, or labels
- Search queries or browsing history
- Chat conversations
- Location data
- Contact information
- Biometric data

### Data Processed On-Device Only
The following data is processed entirely on your device and never transmitted:
- **Photo analysis**: ML classification, vision language model analysis, and OCR text extraction all run on-device using your phone's processor
- **Search index**: Semantic search vectors and keyword indexes are stored locally
- **Chat history**: All AI chat conversations are stored on your device only
- **Workflow results**: All workflow execution data remains on your device
- **Expense records**: Receipt scanning and expense tracking data stays on your device

### Data We May Collect (Opt-In Only)
If you explicitly opt in via Settings > Privacy:
- **Anonymous usage analytics**: App feature usage patterns (e.g., which screens are visited, feature frequency). No photo content, no personal identifiers.
- **Crash reports**: Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs. No personal data included.

You can disable analytics and crash reporting at any time in Settings > Privacy.

### Subscription Data
When you subscribe to Aura Edge AI Premium:
- Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) processes your payment. We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card number, or billing address.
- We receive a transaction confirmation from Apple/Google to verify your subscription status. This is used solely to unlock premium features.

## Camera and Photo Library Access

- **Camera**: Used to capture photos for AI analysis within the app. Photos are processed locally and never transmitted.
- **Photo Library**: Used to access your existing photos for AI analysis, search, and organization. Photos are processed locally and never transmitted.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:
- On-device databases (Isar, ObjectBox) for structured data
- iOS Keychain / Android Keystore for sensitive tokens
- SharedPreferences for app settings

No data is stored on external servers or cloud services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no:
- Advertising networks or ad tracking
- Third-party analytics services (unless you opt in)
- Data brokers or data sales
- Social media integrations that share your data

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time:
- **Settings > Storage > Clear All Data** removes all analysis results, search indexes, chat history, and cached data
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy

Aura Edge AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
